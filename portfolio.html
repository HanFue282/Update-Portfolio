Artwork


I like to draw something that is out of this world. Mostly, something from space or something that is from my dreams.

This art piece is called Galaxy based on one of my dreams, I was traveling somewhere between the stars until I came face to face with the blackhole. I felt it trying to pull me into the unknown abyss. My vision, once filled with stars spreading far and wide, became subcumb to darkness and in my vision the stars disappear. I then hear a bird. I woke up to a bird waking up in the morning.
